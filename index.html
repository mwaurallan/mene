about Menengai farmers

Menengai Farmers is situated along Limuru,Banana Road. At Tigoni Kamonde area Karabaini sub -location.The former owner of the farm mr Collin Campbell had named it Maraba Estate,a name which was derived from the name Karamba-ini.

The Journey

Farm was acquired in 1964 with its oriinal shareholders among others Joseph Kuria Tharao,James Waiganjo Gitungo Evans Kamau Munira,Mwangi Mbothu,Gorge Njau Mwagiru,Johnson Kagua Kiarie,And Elijah Mwangi Mbiru

Farm History

At the point of purchase and incorporation the farm had 130 herds of cattle and 57 acres was under Tea zoned as Tigoni,House,Tank and Rwaka.Tea planting commenced immediately by planting Kofia and some parts of kinuthia.

Grownth and Expansion

Menengai farmers has grown over the years and key among its progress marks is the acquisations of Maramba Tea Factory. The factory has fully maximized our production grownth by ensuring overproduction doesnt go to waste and our quality tea is added value from the source.

Achievements and Milestones

  1. No Farmer has lost his/her crop as we incorporated our partner processing factory
  2. Farmers have increased tea acreage to assured processing capabilities
  3. Company has expanded and ventured into modern dairy farming both for cattle and sheeps

Our Products & Services

Why you choose Menengai Farmers

Quality Highland Tea

Menengai Farmers Limited have over 60 years of experience in the growing and management of high quality tea. They have a strong tea heritage that gives Our Tea consumers confidence that they are taking the best tea.

High Yield Dairly Animals

We are a modern farm that Breads high milk yielding cows, with full-time skilled employees who are trained and equipped with modern farming techniques. We supply milk and heifers country wide , Our target clients are households, farmers, groceries, shops, and mini supermarkets.
